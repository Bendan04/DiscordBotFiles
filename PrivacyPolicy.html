Privacy Policy

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data our bot collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

When using our bot, the following data may be collected:

  • Discord user IDs for command processing and moderation purposes.
  • Server IDs to provide features specific to your server.
  • Message content for temporary processing (e.g., executing code snippets).

Data Usage

The collected data is used exclusively for providing the bot's functionality and is not shared with third parties.

Data Retention

We do not store user data long-term. Any temporary data is cleared once processing is complete.

Data Removal

If you wish to remove your data or stop using the bot, please remove the bot from your server and contact us for further assistance.

Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the bot implies acceptance of the updated policy.
